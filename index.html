How It Works

  • Step 1: Portfolios

    A portfolio is a container for all proposals you want to be rated and voted on a specfic way. Think of it as a way to categorize your proposals.

  • Step 2: Factors

    Factors are the criteria you will use to rank each proposal. Each factor can have a list of options that voter will be able to choose from.

  • Step 3: Proposals

    Once the group is made available to your team, users can submit proposals for voting. Proposals can be links to other web pages or uploaded documents.

  • Step 4: Discussions

    Voters and followers have the opportunity to review each proposal and ask questions in discussion forums to clarify their understanding.

  • Step 5: Voting

    Voting consists of ranking each proposal based upon the factors provided. Previously ranked proposals can be re-prioritized as new proposals are submitted.

  • Step 6: Analysis

    Utilize business intelligence to identify trends, opporunities, and warnings automatically to help everyone make the best decisions possible.
